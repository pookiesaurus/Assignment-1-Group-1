Match

Now you know how to Game & Set you can now win a Match:

The best of three or five sets wins the Match
Note: matches are comprised of games and sets therefore you must win 6 games with
a margin of at least 2 sets. Additionally, the 3 or 5 (depending on gender) sets
need to be won

There are a few diffrent types of matches that can be played these include:

  1. Male singles - 2 male players play in the singles sideline
  2. Female singles - 2 female players play in the singles sideline
  3. Male doubles - 4 male players play in the doubles sideline
  4. Female doubles - 4 female players play in the doubles sideline
